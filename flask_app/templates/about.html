About

This is a basic (reserved) Flask app that displays statistical information for all current NFL players as well as information about their fantasy value. A review system is also in place so users can provide reviews about players. You will notice immediately that the data overall seems to be lacking. This is the case 100%. The reason for it is the API we decided to obtain our data from. Statistical football data is apparently not always free and we had a difficult time obtaining it. This API, though only providing limited data, would serve our purpose if we were to expand our obtainable data outside of just the test data.

This Flask app was created by Viraj Patel, Henry Wang and Gordon Wu.
